Our Mission

We understand that budgeting can be time-consuming, boring, and overwhelming. At Pancake, our mission is not to teach you how to budget or provide the tools to do it yourself. We're here to automate the process and take it out of your hands completely. No more spreadsheets, stress, or guesswork. We want to help you take control of your finances and start saving money — one stack at a time.
